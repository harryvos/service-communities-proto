Learn about the phases of a community

We use the below to assess maturity of a community and help you assess what to do next

  • {{ govukTag({ text: "discovery" }) }}

    Communities which are in the discovery phase are relatively new and have held 1-3 workshops. In this phase communities should:

    • Define the scope of the service area
    • Identify required departments, agencies and third parties
    • Share a community proposal document
    • Draft community terms of reference
    • Draft service landscape

  • {{ govukTag({ text: "alpha" }) }}

    For your community to be in this stage, you should:

    • Make a map
    • Publish a step-by-step

  • {{ govukTag({ text: "beta" }) }}

    For your community to be in this stage, you should:

    • Share research
    • Share data

  • {{ govukTag({ text: "live" }) }}

    For your community to be in this stage, you should:

    • Make a cross-government business case
